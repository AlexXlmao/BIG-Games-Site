My Restaurant - Superhero Update!

Attention all heros and heroines! The My Restaurant Superhero update has arrived! Get ready for our newest addition, the Superhero Customer! Along with heroic items, trading and much more. Embark on a culinary journey and unleash your inner hero!

 

 

Superhero Update!

 

 

Featuring

 

New Superhero Customer!

Power up! A heroic Superhero customer has arrived…

Saving the day sure does work up an appetite!

Look out for this new customer in your restaurant!

 

New Superhero Restaurant Theme!

Explore the all-new Superhero restaurant theme. 

The new Superhero theme is great whether you’re fighting villains or just flying by for a bite to eat!

 

New Superhero Chair!

Introducing the mighty Superhero chair!

Check out the new Superhero Chair now in the shop!  

This new item will attract the Superhero Customer to your restaurant!

 

New Superhero Table!

Elevate your dining experience to super heights…

Try the all new Superhero Table, fit for the mightiest of champions!

This new item will attract the Superhero Customer to your restaurant!

 

New Telephone Booth!

Justice calls…

Answer the call with the all new Telephone Booth item!

Increase the chances that a Superhero customer will visit your restaurant by displaying the Telephone Booth item!

 

New Alter Ego Display Case!

Experience the all new Alter Ego Display Case! 

Whether you're a caped crusader or a “regular guy”… 

The Alter Ego Display Case is the perfect way to showcase your alter ego identity with pride! 

This item increases the chance by up to 50% that a Superhero pays 2x!  

 

Trading and all new Trading Plaza!

The moment we have all been waiting for…

Trading is now in My Restaurant!!!

Trade between players in your server or visit the all new Trading Plaza to meet new people and trade for even better items!

 

Changes

- Character and gameplay bug fixes

- New “Manage” tab now contains all restaurant related information (Staff, Customers, Recipes)

- New Superhero Event Space

- Map Optimization


 

 

Exciting updates are on the way for My Restaurant, enjoy! ~ Preston & MR Dev Team
